Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Information Collection

UBUNTU Tennis Matcher is a static web application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or match data to any external servers. All data you enter (player names, scores, etc.) is stored exclusively in your browser's Local Storage.

2. Use of Data

The data stored in your browser is used solely to provide the functionality of the matching system, such as maintaining player lists and calculating statistics for your current session.

3. Data Retention

Data remains in your browser until you manually clear it using the "Reset All Arena Data" button within the app or by clearing your browser's site data/cache.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.
